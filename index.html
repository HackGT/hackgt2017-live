HackGT CTF

This year, HackGT4 will be hosting a special Capture the Flag mini-game, running from midnight on Saturday till the end of the event!

First, what is a CTF? Normally, a CTF contains a variety of puzzles and challenges that can be completing by finding a flag. These flags are typically given at the completion of a challenge. These challenges are typically security focused with challenges such as physical, forensics, web exploitation, and binary exploitation. However, in the spirit of HackGT, we have created a wide variety of fun problems related to various protocols, procedures, and libraries that are typically used in Hackathons. This includes things like asymmetric cryptography, image processing, and the blockchain. We encourage everyone to have fun, try a challenge or two, and learn something new this weekend!
